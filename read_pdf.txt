Subject: Request for IVR Outbound Call Campaign – Early Bird Property Tax Scheme 2026-27
 
 
Dear Sir/Madam,
 
Greetings from GHMC!
 
This is to inform you that the Hon'ble Commissioner, GHMC has desired to launch an outbound call
campaign targeting citizens who paid their property tax last year but have not yet paid for the current year
2026-27.
 
The objective of the campaign is to appeal to these citizens to avail the Early Bird Scheme and pay their
property tax before 30th April 2026 to avail a 5% rebate on current year tax including arrears, if any.
 
The approximate number of mobile numbers identified for this campaign is 6.5 Lakh (as on date).
 
The proposed message content for the IVR calls is as follows:
 
"Dear Citizen, Please avail Early Bird Scheme and get 5% rebate on current year tax i.e 2026-27 against
PTIN : 1051401165 , HNO: '14-10-729 by paying before 30th April 2026 including arrears if any.You can
pay your tax at (GHMC,CMC,MMC) Citizen Service Centers, Bill Collectors, Meeseva or make payment
Online through GHMC, CMC, MMC websites/ MyCURE app / UPI apps like Gpay, Phone pay etc. or by
clicking on below link https://onlinepayments.ghmc.gov.in/PTax/getPTaxDemandDetails?
XYZ=E1D0F9EB9F872C4EE11D02D29F9B0E10 Note : Secured cashless payment methods ONLY
enabled for Property Tax Collection. Go Cashless - Save Time. -Team CURE"
 
In view of the above, GVK – EMRI Green Health Services is requested to kindly provide a suitable solution
and proposal for executing IVR outbound calls for approximately 6.5 Lakh mobile numbers at the earliest.
 
It is further requested to ensure that the entire campaign is completed before 30th April 2026.
 
Your prompt action in this regard is highly solicited.
 
Regards,
Sairam,
